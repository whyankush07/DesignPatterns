It defines a family of algorithm, put them in a separate class so that they can be changed at runtime.

e.g.: the algorithms (methods), which can be changed in future, put them in a separate abstract class
      and let the polymorphism play it's game by linking them with composition.

Note: Solution of inheritance cannot be more inheritance.
Tip:  Prefer composition over inheritance.

Conclusions:

1. Encapsulate what varies and keep it separate from what remains same
2. Do Not Repeat Yourself (DRY)
